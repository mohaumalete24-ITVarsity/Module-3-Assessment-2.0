Maltesers-tiramisu

prep time: 2 hr 00

yield: 4

ingredients








steps

  1. Whisk thickened cream, mascarpone and caster sugar in a bowl until soft peaks form.
  2. Combine freshly brewed espresso coffee and Kahlua liqueur.
  3. Chop savoiardi. Divide half the biscuit among four 1 1/2 cup glasses. Drizzle over half the coffee mixture. Top each with 8 Maltesers. Divide half the mascarpone mixture among glasses. Dust with cocoa powder. Top with the remaining biscuit, coffee mixture and mascarpone mixture. Top with extra Maltesers. Chill for 2-3 hours. Dust with extra cocoa powder.
