Dolce-di-ricotta

prep time: 25min

yield: 6

ingredients
















steps

  1. To make pastry shell, combine flour and sugar in a large bowl. Add eggs, extract and zest. Mix to form a soft dough. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and shape into a disc. Wrap in cling film and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. Bring dough to room temperature then roll out on a lightly floured surface until about 3mm thick. Use to line the base and side of a deep 22cm round cake pan with removable base.
  3. Preheat oven to 180C.
  4. To make filling, whisk ricotta until smooth. Whisk in sugar. Whisk in remaining ingredients. Pour into cooled pastry shell. Dust lightly with cinnamon.
  5. Bake for about 45 minutes or until filling is just set. Cool to room temperature.
